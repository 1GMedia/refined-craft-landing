## üìù Copywriting Strategy

Applied principles from copywriting skill:

### Clarity Over Cleverness
- "Never watch a course just to forget it" (clear pain point)
- Not: "Revolutionize your learning experience" (vague)

### Benefits Over Features
- "Turn 4-hour courses into 8-minute action plans"
- Not: "Advanced AI transcription and summarization"

### Specificity Over Vagueness
- "Chat with AI trained on 1,000+ courses"
- "Runs 100% locally on your machine"
- "Extract frameworks, not just highlights"

### Customer Language
- "Never watch a course just to forget it"
- "Information overload"
- "No bridge to action"

### Page Structure
1. Hero: Pain point + solution
2. Problem: Show understanding
3. Solution: Connect to outcomes
4. How It Works: Reduce complexity
5. Use Cases: Prove application
6. Differentiation: Clear comparison
7. Final CTA: Recap + action
